How To Make a Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheese
Spread butter on bread and frying pan, Place bread in frying pan and fry, Add cheese on top of bread, Cover with second slice of bread, Turn over and fry for 2 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
